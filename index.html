Top Water | Top Field Force (TFF)

The project involves developing a comprehensive tracking and analysis system for Top Water Company's marketing team. It consists of a mobile app for user registration and real-time location tracking, and an admin site for managing users and analyzing their performance. Users register through the app using an OTP from the admin, who approves access via the admin site. The app records and transmits location data, categorized into Morning, Afternoon, and Overtime Hours. Admins can then log in, view detailed movement data, and evaluate the distance traveled and locations visited by each team member, ensuring accountability and optimizing marketing efforts.
